About Me

I am a fifth year graduate student in mathematics studying probability and stochastic processes at the University of Arizona. Before I became a graduate student, I was a product engineer and pre-silicon validation engineer at Intel Corporation from 2011 to 2016. I received my M.S.E in Electrical Engineering from Arizona State in 2012 and B.Sc in Mathematics from the University of Arizona in 2021. I am interested in the connection and applications of probability and stochastic processes in mathematics and other fields, including interacting particle system, stochastic partial differential equation, artificial intelligence. I am also interested in numerical simulations of equations with singular coefficients.
