About Me

I am a fifth year graduate student in mathematics studying probability and stochastic processes at the University of Arizona. Before I became a graduate student, I was a product engineer and pre-silicon validation engineer at Intel Corp. from 2011 to 2016. I received my MSE in Electrical Engineering from Arizona State in 2012 and BSc in Mathematics from the University of Arizona in 2021. I am interested in the connection and application of probability and stochastic processes in mathematics and other fields of study, including interacting particle system, Brox diffusion, stochastic partial differential equation, stochastic optimization, and in general, connections to Artificial Intelligence.
